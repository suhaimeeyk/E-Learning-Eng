CUZCO, PERU
3. The fabulous Inti Raymi, or Sun Festival, is the second biggest festival in Latin America after Carnaval in Brazil.
4. The festival goes back about 500 years, to Inca times. The Inca believed that they were descendants of the sun. Inti Raymi was the time when they said, " Sun! We love you! Please don't leave us! "
5. Nowadays, hundreds of people act out the roles of Inca priests, nobles, and soldiers. The ceremony starts in the main square. After that, thousands of people follow the actors to an ancient fortress in the hills. Later, there's great food, music, and dancing!
